## Architecture Decision Records (ADR)

## Overview

Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) document significant technical, architectural, educational, and organizational decisions made by Code and Core.

Each ADR captures:

- The problem or context
- The decision made
- The rationale behind the decision
- Alternatives considered
- Consequences of the decision

ADRs preserve institutional knowledge, improve transparency, and help future contributors understand why important decisions were made.

---

## ADR Lifecycle

```text
Decision Identified
        ↓
Research
        ↓
Evaluate Alternatives
        ↓
Decision Proposed
        ↓
ADR Written
        ↓
Review
        ↓
Accepted
        ↓
Implementation
        ↓
Future Review (if needed)
```
