# Company Handbook

Welcome to the **Code and Core University Company Handbook**.

This handbook serves as the central reference for the purpose, direction, principles, and operating standards of Code and Core University.

Whether you are a learner, instructor, contributor, or future team member, this handbook explains how the organization is designed, what it stands for, and how decisions are made.

---

## Purpose

The Company Handbook provides a shared understanding of the organization's long-term vision and operating philosophy.

It ensures consistency across courses, projects, documentation, community activities, and future products.

---

## What You'll Find

This section includes:

- Mission
- Vision
- Core Values
- Brand Identity
- Teaching Philosophy
- Learning Philosophy
- Quality Standards
- Long-Term Roadmap

Each document builds upon the previous one and together forms the foundation of Code and Core University.
