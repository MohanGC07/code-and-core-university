## Core Values

## Purpose

This document defines the core values of Code and Core University. These values guide our educational philosophy, business decisions, community culture, and long-term growth.

---

## Our Core Values

## 1. Education First

Learning outcomes always come before revenue, popularity, or growth. Every decision should improve the learner's experience and success.

---

## 2. Learn by Building

We believe the best way to learn technology is by creating real projects. Every learner should gain practical experience, not just theoretical knowledge.

---

## 3. Accessible Learning

We strive to make high-quality technology education available to learners from all backgrounds through free resources, structured documentation, and affordable learning opportunities.

---

## 4. Quality Without Compromise

We publish educational content only when it meets our standards for clarity, accuracy, relevance, and practical value.

---

## 5. Integrity & Honesty

We communicate honestly, acknowledge mistakes, credit original sources, and continuously improve our content based on feedback and new knowledge.

---

## 6. Lifelong Learning

Technology evolves constantly. We encourage curiosity, continuous improvement, and a growth mindset for both learners and educators.

---

## 7. Open Source First

Whenever practical, we prioritize free and open-source technologies, encouraging learners to build with accessible tools while understanding when commercial solutions are appropriate.

---

## 8. Ethical AI & Technology

We promote the responsible use of technology by considering privacy, security, fairness, and the broader impact of our work on society.

---

## 9. Community Before Competition

We believe learning grows stronger through collaboration, mutual respect, and knowledge sharing. We encourage learners to support one another and celebrate collective success.

---

## Living Our Values

These values are not slogans. They are principles that shape every course, lesson, project, video, partnership, and future product developed by Code and Core University.
