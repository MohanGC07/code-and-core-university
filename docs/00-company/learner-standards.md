## Learner Standards

## Purpose

This document defines the expectations, responsibilities, and professional behaviors of every learner at Code and Core University.

The purpose of these standards is to create a learning culture where learners take ownership of their growth, collaborate with integrity, embrace continuous improvement, and prepare themselves for successful careers and lifelong learning.

These standards apply to all learners, including school students, college students, working professionals, career changers, corporate trainees, bootcamp participants, and self-paced learners.

---

## Learner Responsibility

Every learner is expected to take ownership of their learning journey.

Learners are responsible for:

- Preparing before learning sessions.
- Participating actively.
- Practicing consistently.
- Building projects.
- Asking thoughtful questions.
- Applying feedback.
- Reflecting on mistakes.
- Continuing to learn beyond the course.

Learning is a partnership. Instructors create opportunities to learn, while learners take responsibility for learning.

---

## Growth Mindset

Code and Core learners approach challenges with curiosity, resilience, and a commitment to continuous improvement.

Learners should:

- Embrace challenges.
- Learn from mistakes.
- Welcome constructive feedback.
- Practice consistently.
- Reflect on progress.
- Stay curious.
- Continue learning beyond the course.

Progress is achieved through deliberate practice, reflection, and continuous improvement rather than perfection.

---

## Professional Learning Habits

Learners are encouraged to develop habits that reflect the mindset of technology professionals.

Recommended habits include:

- Prepare before each lesson.
- Take meaningful notes.
- Practice beyond the provided examples.
- Build projects consistently.
- Read official documentation.
- Use Git for version control.
- Review previous lessons regularly.
- Reflect on completed work.
- Share knowledge with others when appropriate.

Professional habits are developed through consistent daily practice.

---

## Collaborative Learning

Technology is built through collaboration.

Learners are encouraged to:

- Participate in discussions.
- Ask and answer thoughtful questions.
- Join study groups.
- Review peers' work respectfully.
- Pair program when appropriate.
- Share useful resources.
- Learn from others while producing original work.

Collaboration should strengthen understanding while maintaining personal accountability.

### Guiding Principle

> Learn together. Build with integrity.

---

## Academic Integrity and Responsible AI Use

Integrity is essential to professional growth.

Learners are expected to produce original work, understand the solutions they submit, and use AI responsibly.

## Responsible AI Framework

```text
Think
   ↓
Attempt
   ↓
AI
   ↓
Verify
   ↓
Explain
   ↓
Improve
```

### Responsible AI Practices

- Use AI to learn concepts and explore ideas.
- Use AI to debug and improve understanding.
- Verify all AI-generated outputs.
- Be able to explain submitted work.
- Credit external sources when appropriate.
- Respect licenses and intellectual property.
- Avoid plagiarism and misrepresentation.

AI should accelerate learning, never replace thinking.

---

## Professional Conduct

Every learner is expected to demonstrate professionalism throughout their learning journey.

Professional expectations include:

- Communicate respectfully and professionally.
- Arrive prepared and participate actively.
- Meet agreed deadlines whenever possible.
- Accept constructive feedback.
- Respect instructors, peers, and the learning environment.
- Act with honesty and integrity.
- Take responsibility for actions and commitments.
- Represent Code and Core positively.

Professionalism begins during learning—not after employment.

---

## Learn, Build, and Contribute

Learning becomes stronger when knowledge is shared.

Learners are encouraged to contribute according to their experience by:

- Asking thoughtful questions.
- Helping fellow learners.
- Sharing useful resources.
- Presenting projects.
- Writing technical notes or articles.
- Creating tutorials.
- Contributing to open-source projects.
- Mentoring beginners when ready.
- Participating in community discussions.

As learners grow, they are encouraged to help others grow as well.

### Core Message

> As you grow, help others grow with you.

---

## Benefits

Following these standards helps learners:

- Develop professional habits.
- Build confidence through practice.
- Create stronger project portfolios.
- Collaborate effectively.
- Use AI responsibly.
- Prepare for internships, employment, entrepreneurship, and lifelong learning.

---

## Implementation Examples

## Self-Paced Learner

- Plans weekly study sessions.
- Builds projects beyond course requirements.
- Uses AI responsibly to understand concepts.
- Shares notes with the community.

## Bootcamp Learner

- Participates actively in discussions.
- Pair programs with classmates.
- Applies instructor feedback to improve projects.
- Demonstrates solutions during project reviews.

## Corporate Learner

- Completes practical exercises.
- Collaborates respectfully with colleagues.
- Applies new skills to workplace challenges.
- Reflects on learning outcomes and future improvements.

Although learning environments differ, the learner standards remain consistent.
