## Grade 8 Semester 2 Learning Plan

## Purpose

Semester 2 focuses on intelligent systems engineering. Students integrate programming, Artificial Intelligence, robotics, cybersecurity, and engineering design to develop practical technology solutions for real-world problems.

---

## Semester Theme

## Building Intelligent Systems*

Students learn to:

- Apply Artificial Intelligence concepts.
- Design intelligent robotics systems.
- Understand cybersecurity fundamentals.
- Integrate hardware and software.
- Develop engineering solutions collaboratively.

---

## Semester Structure

|Weeks|Unit|Focus|
|-----|----|-----|
|19–22|Unit 5|Artificial Intelligence Workflow|
|23–27|Unit 6|Robotics & Intelligent Automation|
|28–32|Unit 7|Computer Systems & Cybersecurity|
|33–36|Unit 8|Innovation Capstone Project|

---

## Unit 5 — Artificial Intelligence Workflow

## Learning Objectives

Students will:

- Understand the AI development lifecycle.
- Explain machine learning concepts.
- Recognize the importance of quality datasets.
- Practice responsible AI use.
- Develop effective prompts for AI tools.

### Topics

- Artificial Intelligence
- Machine Learning
- Deep Learning (Introduction)
- Data Collection
- Data Preparation
- Model Training
- Model Evaluation
- Prompt Engineering
- Responsible AI

### Practical Activities

- AI Image Classification Demo
- Prompt Engineering Challenge
- Dataset Exploration
- AI Ethics Discussion
- AI Case Study Presentation

### Deliverables

- AI Reflection Report
- Prompt Collection
- Group Presentation

---

## Unit 6 — Robotics & Intelligent Automation

## Learning Objectives — Robotics & Intelligent Automation

Students will:

- Build multi-sensor robotic systems.
- Program automation logic.
- Integrate sensors and actuators.
- Troubleshoot robotics systems.
- Document engineering solutions.

### Topics — Robotics & Intelligent Automation

- Multi-Sensor Systems
- Servo Motors
- LCD Displays
- Ultrasonic Sensors
- Environmental Sensors
- Automation Logic
- Introduction to IoT

### Practical Activities — Robotics & Intelligent Automation

- Smart Parking System
- Smart Door Alarm
- Automatic Plant Watering System
- Weather Monitoring Device
- Smart Classroom Prototype

### Deliverables — Robotics & Intelligent Automation

- Arduino Programs
- Circuit Diagrams
- Engineering Notebook
- Demonstration Video

---

## Unit 7 — Computer Systems & Cybersecurity

## Learning Objectives — Computer Systems & Cybersecurity

Students will:

- Understand operating system functions.
- Explain networking fundamentals.
- Apply cybersecurity best practices.
- Protect digital information.
- Practice responsible online behavior.

### Topics — Computer Systems & Cybersecurity

- Operating Systems
- Computer Networks
- Internet Architecture
- Password Security
- Cyber Threats
- Digital Privacy
- Safe Internet Practices

### Practical Activities — Computer Systems & Cybersecurity

- Password Security Audit
- Network Mapping Exercise
- Cybersecurity Awareness Campaign
- Digital Safety Poster

### Deliverables — Computer Systems & Cybersecurity

- Cybersecurity Report
- Network Diagram
- Awareness Presentation

---

## Unit 8 — Innovation Capstone Project

## Learning Objectives — Innovation Capstone Project

Students will:

- Apply knowledge from the entire academic year.
- Work effectively in engineering teams.
- Build complete technology solutions.
- Present projects professionally.

### Suggested Projects

- Smart Classroom
- AI Study Assistant
- School Information System
- Smart Home Prototype
- IoT Monitoring System
- Smart Attendance System

### Required Deliverables

- Project Proposal
- Design Documentation
- Source Code
- Hardware Prototype (if applicable)
- User Guide
- Testing Report
- Final Presentation

## Teaching Methodology

Teachers should emphasize:

- Inquiry-Based Learning
- Engineering Design Process
- Team Collaboration
- Design Thinking
- Prototype Development
- Continuous Testing
- Reflection and Improvement

## Expected Outcomes

By the end of Semester 2, students should be able to:

- Explain the Artificial Intelligence workflow.
- Develop intelligent robotics systems.
- Apply cybersecurity best practices.
- Integrate hardware and software solutions.
- Design engineering projects collaboratively.
- Present technical work professionally.

---

## Progression to Grade 9

Students successfully completing Semester 2 will be prepared for:

- Advanced Python Programming
- Data Structures
- Databases
- Full-Stack Web Development
- Machine Learning Foundations
- Internet of Things
- Software Architecture
- Engineering Research
