## Grade 8 Yearly Learning Plan

## Purpose

The Grade 8 Yearly Learning Plan provides a structured roadmap for delivering the curriculum over one academic year. The curriculum gradually transitions students from intermediate computing skills toward software engineering and intelligent system development.

---

## Academic Calendar

|Duration|Value|
|--------|-----|
|Academic Year|12 Months|
|Semesters|2|
|Weeks|36 Teaching Weeks|
|Weekly Sessions|5|
|Practical Learning|70%|
|Theory|30%|

---

## Annual Learning Themes

## Semester 1

## Professional Software Development

Students strengthen programming, web development, software engineering, and collaborative development practices.

---

## Semester 2

## Intelligent Systems Engineering

Students integrate Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, IoT concepts, cybersecurity, and engineering design into complete technology solutions.

## Expected Graduate Profile

Upon successful completion of Grade 8, students should demonstrate:

- Strong programming skills.
- Responsive web development competency.
- Practical AI knowledge.
- Robotics engineering skills.
- Professional documentation habits.
- Collaborative software development.
- Engineering problem-solving.
- Ethical technology practices.
