## Purpose

The Curriculum Master Plan provides a unified educational roadmap that guides learners from Grade 5 to Grade 12.

Rather than teaching isolated subjects, Code and Core develops future-ready learners through a continuous, project-based, technology-focused education.

---

## Vision

Every learner graduates with the knowledge, practical experience, portfolio, and professional mindset required to succeed in university, entrepreneurship, software engineering, artificial intelligence, robotics, or future technology careers.

---

## Educational Philosophy

Learning follows one continuous cycle.

```text
Learn
   ↓
Practice
   ↓
Build
   ↓
Reflect
   ↓
Improve
   ↓
Share
   ↓
Lead
```

Students learn by building.

## Annual Learning Model

Every academic year contains:

- Core Lessons
- Guided Practice
- Weekly Labs
- Monthly Mini Projects
- Semester Projects
- Assessments
- Portfolio Updates
- Reflection

---

## Graduation Requirements

To graduate, learners should:

- Complete all required courses.
- Pass assessments.
- Maintain a professional portfolio.
- Complete a capstone project.
- Present their work publicly.
- Demonstrate ethical and professional conduct.
