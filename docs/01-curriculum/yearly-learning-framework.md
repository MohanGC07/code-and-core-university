## Yearly Learning Framework

---

## Purpose

Every grade follows the same academic structure.

A consistent framework ensures:

- Predictable learning progression
- High instructional quality
- Easier curriculum maintenance
- Consistent learner experience
- Standardized assessments

---

## Academic Year Structure

The academic year consists of:

Semester 1

↓

Mid-Year Review

↓

Semester 2

↓

Final Portfolio Review

↓

Promotion

---

## Semester Structure

Each semester contains:

- Learning Units
- Practical Labs
- Mini Projects
- Assessments
- Reflection
- Portfolio Update

---

## Monthly Learning Cycle

Week 1

Concept Learning

Week 2

Guided Practice

Week 3

Hands-on Lab

Week 4

Mini Project + Assessment

---

## Lesson Structure

Every lesson follows the same format.

1. Learning Objectives

2. Prerequisites

3. Theory

4. Demonstration

5. Guided Practice

6. Independent Practice

7. Challenge Activity

8. Reflection

9. Homework

10. Portfolio Evidence

---

## Practical Learning Ratio

Theory

30%

Practice

70%

Students learn primarily by building.

---

## Assessment Structure

Assessment includes:

- Weekly Activities
- Monthly Mini Projects
- Semester Project
- Portfolio Review
- Practical Examination
- Presentation

---

## Project-Based Learning

Every semester contains:

- 4 Mini Projects
- 1 Major Project

Projects should solve meaningful problems whenever possible.

---

## Portfolio Development

Every learner maintains a digital portfolio containing:

- Programming Projects
- Robotics Projects
- AI Projects
- Reports
- Documentation
- Presentations
- GitHub Repositories
- Certificates

---

## Skills Assessment

Students are evaluated in:

Technical Skills

Problem Solving

Communication

Teamwork

Creativity

Professionalism

Ethics

---

## Parent Engagement

Parents receive:

- Progress Reports
- Portfolio Reviews
- Semester Demonstrations
- Learning Recommendations

---

## Instructor Responsibilities

Every instructor should:

- Follow the curriculum
- Encourage creativity
- Provide timely feedback
- Assess fairly
- Mentor learners
- Promote ethical technology use

---

## Annual Capstone

Each grade concludes with:

- Final Project
- Presentation
- Portfolio Showcase

---

## Continuous Improvement

The framework is reviewed annually using:

- Student feedback
- Parent feedback
- Instructor feedback
- Industry trends
- Academic research
