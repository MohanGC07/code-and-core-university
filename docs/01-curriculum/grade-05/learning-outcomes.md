## Grade 5 Learning Outcomes

## Purpose

Define what every learner should know and be able to do by the end of Grade 5.

---

## Digital Literacy

Students will be able to:

- Operate a computer safely.
- Use a keyboard and mouse confidently.
- Create and organize files.
- Practice safe internet habits.

---

## Computational Thinking

Students will be able to:

- Recognize patterns.
- Break problems into smaller steps.
- Design simple algorithms.
- Debug simple programs.

---

## Programming

Students will:

- Create Scratch programs.
- Build animations.
- Create interactive games.
- Use variables.
- Use loops.
- Use conditions.

---

## Artificial Intelligence

Students will:

- Explain AI in simple words.
- Identify AI used in daily life.
- Discuss responsible AI use.

---

## Robotics

Students will:

- Identify basic robot components.
- Build simple electronic circuits.
- Explain sensors and outputs.

---

## Professional Skills

Students will:

- Work in teams.
- Present projects.
- Communicate ideas clearly.
- Respect digital ethics.
