## Grade 5 Learning Blueprint

## Theme

### Discover Technology

Students begin exploring computers through creativity, curiosity, and hands-on activities.

---

## Learning Goals

By the end of Grade 5, learners will:

- Use computers confidently.
- Understand basic digital safety.
- Think logically.
- Create simple programs.
- Build basic robotics projects.
- Work collaboratively.
- Present their ideas.
